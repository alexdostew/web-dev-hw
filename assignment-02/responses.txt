Briefly describe one way how data travels on the internet.

Data travels on the internet by being broken down into small "packets" and sent through routers
which tell the packets which routes to take to reach the intended internet address where they are
then reassembled and checked through transmission control protocol.


What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?

an IP address is a unique number given to each device on the network which acts like a "mailing address" for data.
the DNS associates names with a corresponding address. Your computer uses the DNS to look up domain names and obtain
the IP address that you are trying to connect to.


What is a cookie?

Cookies are what websites use to remember who you are. It is basically an ID number stored in your web browser.
