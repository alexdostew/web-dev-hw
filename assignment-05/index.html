Pumpkin Cake Recipe

Image source

Ingredients

Cake

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups pumpkin
  • 1 cup vegetable oil or applesauce
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Frosting

  • 1 8oz package of cream cheese (softened)
  • 6 tbsp butter (softened)
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 4 cups powdered sugar

Directions

Cake

  1. Combine the 8 cake ingredients in a bowl
  2. Pour into a 9x13 pan that has been greased and floured
  3. Bake 325 degrees for 35 min or until a toothpick or knife inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean

Frosting

  1. Mix cream cheese, butter and vanilla together
  2. Add the powdered sugar one cup at a time
  3. spread on cooled cake
